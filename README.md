# Quentin Macé
**PhD Student on Multi-Agent systems at Illuin Technology & CentraleSupélec (MICS) & University of Grenoble (LIG)**

Welcome to my academic page! I am a first-year industrial PhD student at Illuin Technology working conjointly with CentraleSupélec (MICS LAB) & University of Grenoble (LIG Lab), advised by Céline Hudelot and Maxime Peyrard. 

My research focuses on robustness for multi-agent systems. Specifically, I am interested in exploring how we can evaluate and design agentic systems that are robust to perturbations such as adversarial attacks, tool errors or simple mistakes made by individual agents.
