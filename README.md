# Quentin Macé
**PhD Student in Multi-Agent Systems** | *Illuin Technology, CentraleSupélec (MICS), & University of Grenoble (LIG)*

Welcome to my academic page! I am a first-year industrial PhD student at Illuin Technology, working conjointly with CentraleSupélec (MICS Lab) and the University of Grenoble (LIG Lab). I am advised by Céline Hudelot and Maxime Peyrard.

> My research focuses on the **robustness of multi-agent systems**. Specifically, I explore how to evaluate and design agentic systems that are resilient to perturbations such as adversarial attacks, tool errors, or simple mistakes made by individual agents.

## 🔬 Research Interests
* **Multi-Agent Systems:** Currently working on my thesis, focusing on systemic robustness and agent evaluation.
* **Multi-Modal Retrieval:** I have worked extensively on multi-modal retrieval, building the latest iterations of the ViDoRe benchmarks and training efficient visual document retrievers.
